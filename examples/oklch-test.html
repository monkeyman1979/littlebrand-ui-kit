Benefits of OKLCH

  • Perceptual Uniformity: OKLCH lightness corresponds directly to perceived brightness
  • Better Gradients: Color transitions are smoother and more natural
  • Consistent Saturation: Chroma remains consistent when adjusting lightness
  • Wide Gamut: Can represent colors beyond sRGB on capable displays
  • Predictable Adjustments: Changing lightness doesn't affect perceived hue
